Catering

Need Catering?

Here at "My Coffee Shop" we have all of your catering needs catered for (excuse the pun). Whether it is 5 or 500 people, we have the menu to suit. We specialise in Wedding Catering, Corporate Events, BBQs, Office Catering, Christenings, Communions & Confirmations.

Our Kitchen

At "My Coffee Shop" Catering, we have a highly skilled culinary team and trained chefs who are extremely passionate about food. All our food is prepared fresh and sourced from local high quality suppliers.
