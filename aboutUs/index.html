Passion for all!

Passion for Fairtrade & Sustainable Production

We at Bean-Co are committed to providing you the best quality beans, sourced directly from our sole supplier in Columbia. We recognise our role in the fight against climate change and towards sustainable development. Running an eco-friendly, green and sustainable business is a core company value and has been a key objective for many years.

Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. From farming techniques, to looking after the environment and supporting the local economy, it supports the community as a whole. In partnership with Manuel and his family run farm, we source only the finest beans direct to ensure quality, environmental standards and fair trade, and deliver on our mission to invest in supporting farming families meet their daily needs.

Passion for You!

Although we are a small family run business, we’re here to help coffee lovers all over Ireland. Our aim is not only to create delicious coffee, but to create an atmosphere and experience that all customer love. We do that with radical customer centricity, doing our best to be on a first name basis with our customers.

Society is rapidly changing. People are moving from the pub to coffee shops in order to socialise. We endeavour to create a space where people can meet, reconnect, or simply relax while enjoying a cup of our famous coffee. Make sure you stop by, we can’t wait to serve you!
